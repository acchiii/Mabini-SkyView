Who We Are

Cebu's Premier
Hilltop Destination

Mabini SkyView was founded in 2018 with a singular purpose: to invite people to slow down, look up, and rediscover the beauty that surrounds Cebu City. Perched atop the hills of Mabini, our destination offers an unobstructed 360-degree view of the Visayan Sea, the city lights below, and the rolling green mountains of Central Visayas.

What started as a family-owned picnic spot has grown into one of Cebu's most beloved tourist destinations — attracting thousands of visitors each year who come seeking fresh air, natural beauty, and a genuine escape from the urban pace.

We are proudly locally owned and operated, deeply committed to sustainable tourism, and passionate about showcasing the hidden natural treasures of Mabini to both local and international visitors.

Our Vision & Mission

Our Vision

To be recognized as Cebu's foremost eco-tourism destination — a sanctuary where nature, culture, and community converge to create transformative experiences for every visitor, while preserving the natural heritage of Mabini for generations to come.

Our Mission

To provide unforgettable, world-class tourist experiences rooted in authentic Filipino hospitality — offering breathtaking views, sustainable nature activities, and curated amenities that leave every guest feeling rested, inspired, and connected to the beauty of Cebu.
